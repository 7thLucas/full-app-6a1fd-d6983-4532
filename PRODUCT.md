# BrewOps - Coffee Shop Operations App

## Product Overview
BrewOps is a coffee shop operations management platform designed to streamline daily operations for coffee shop owners and managers.

## Target Users
- Coffee shop owners and managers
- Baristas and shift supervisors
- Inventory staff

## Brand & Tone
- Brand name: BrewOps
- Tone: Professional yet warm, practical, efficient
- Language: Clear operational language, coffee industry terminology where appropriate

## Core Features

### 1. Bean Inventory Tracking
- Track coffee bean stock levels by variety/origin
- Set minimum stock thresholds per bean type
- Low-stock restock alerts with visual indicators
- Inventory update history log
- Quick restock entry form

### 2. Daily Sales Tracking Dashboard
- Daily revenue totals and transaction counts
- Sales breakdown by product category (espresso drinks, drip, cold brew, food, etc.)
- Hourly sales trend visualization
- Comparison with previous day / week
- Key metrics: average ticket size, top-selling items

### 3. Barista Shift Scheduling
- Weekly shift calendar view
- Assign baristas to shifts with role designation
- Peak-hour visibility (highlight busy periods: morning rush, lunch, afternoon)
- Shift swap / coverage request tracking
- Staffing level vs. expected traffic indicator

## Strategic Principles
- Operations-first: reduce manual work and paper-based tracking
- At-a-glance clarity: dashboards should surface critical info immediately
- Alert-driven: proactively surface low stock and understaffed shifts
- Mobile-friendly for baristas checking schedules on the go

## Anti-References
- Avoid overly complex enterprise UI
- No unnecessary feature bloat
- Keep navigation simple and task-focused

# BrewOps

## Product
BrewOps — an operations app for coffee shop owners to manage daily sales, bean inventory, and barista shifts from one dashboard.

## Core Problem
Coffee shop owners risk running out of beans mid-service and being under/overstaffed during peak hours — both directly hurt revenue and customer experience. Current manual tracking is error-prone and reactive.

## Target Users
Coffee shop owners and managers.

## App Name
BrewOps

## Primary Goals
1. Never run out of beans — real-time inventory tracking with low-stock alerts
2. Optimize peak-hour staffing — shift scheduling tied to sales data
3. Track daily sales — simple daily revenue logging and trends

## MVP Scope
- Bean inventory tracking with low-stock restock alerts (day-one priority)
- Daily sales tracking dashboard
- Barista shift scheduling with peak-hour visibility
